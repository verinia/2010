Welcome to the Jagex Parents' Guide. We are the creators of RuneScape and FunOrb, which, as with many popular online games, attract a wide range of players of many different ages from all around the world. While our games are created with adult gamers in mind, we do appreciate that some younger players also enjoy them.

This guide has been put together for the parents and guardians of these younger players, so that they can learn more about our games, and the active steps we take to ensure account security and online safety.

Our player community is truly global, with over 150 million unique users from all over the world having played Jagex games during the past seven years. We are committed to strong communication with players worldwide, and our games are available in a variety of languages, with fully localised support.

Over half of the Jagex team is dedicated to player support, and their primary responsibility is to maintain a fun and safe gaming environment. The internet never closes, so our multilingual player support team is on hand 24/7 to provide support to players and parents. We have developed in-game content to educate players about online safety and security, and behind the scenes we operate industry-leading technology to prioritise reports from our players.

We work closely with internationally-recognised organisations to ensure that we are providing the highest possible standard of service to all users of our websites and games. To find out more about the organisations that we work with, click here, or click on one of the logos at the bottom of the page to visit each organisation's website.
