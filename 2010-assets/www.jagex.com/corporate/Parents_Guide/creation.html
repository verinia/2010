Any account that your child creates can be used for both RuneScape and FunOrb, so if your child already has a RuneScape account, they can use it to log in to FunOrb and start playing. Creating a RuneScape or FunOrb account is simple! Your child should follow these steps to get started:

Players must create a username: it cannot be offensive, confusing, or contain any form of personal information.

The player needs to choose a suitable password for the account. It should be memorable, and not easy to guess.

Prospective players need to select their age and country of residence from drop-down menus.

The player will need to read and accept the RuneScape or FunOrb Privacy Policy.

The player will need to read the Terms and Conditions and accept them. FunOrb players will then be ready to start enjoying the games.

To read the FunOrb Terms and Condition, click here.
To read the RuneScape Terms and Conditions, click here.

For RuneScape, the final step before beginning the game is creating a character. This involves selecting a gender, the style and colour of the basic clothing, and the hairstyle of the character. As soon as the player is happy with the character, they can enter the RuneScape tutorial, to learn the basics about playing the game and using the controls. Upon completion of the tutorial, the player is then free to roam the game world and begin their adventures.

Click here to go to the RuneScape website, then click on "Create a free account (New user)" to start making a character.

Click here to go to the FunOrb website, then click on "Join" to make a new account.
