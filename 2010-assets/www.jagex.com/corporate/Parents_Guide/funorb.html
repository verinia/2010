When your child visits the FunOrb website, they'll find a variety of free games on offer, from a logic-testing game of chess, to piloting spacecraft, to colourful puzzles. Each of them are deep and massively enjoyable, enticing your child to try them all.

If your child already has a RuneScape account, they can go to the FunOrb website, log in and start playing immediately! Free players can access most game content, highscores tables and many Achievements, and those with the sufficient number of Achievements will be able to access the FunOrb forums. Members have access to the forums no matter how many Achievements they have, and they can also enjoy extra expansions for many of our games.

FunOrb users have access to our forums, where eligible subscribers can discuss the games and the website, and communicate with us.

We also have extra website updates and games planned for the future, with new content every two weeks!
