We offer a variety of methods for subscribing to our games. The following summaries of each payment option will help you to decide on the best method for you.

Runescape and FunOrb players, or parents of players, can subscribe using their credit card. This is usually the cheapest way to subscribe, although the cost depends on the country you are subscribing from. Payments are often, but not always, instantaneous, and are taken once every month until the subscriber decides to cancel. Users can cancel a credit card subscription at any time via the Runescape or FunOrb websites, and the encryption system makes this a very secure payment method. Our PCI DSS compliance ensures the security of your details. This method can be used worldwide by holders of Visa, Visa Delta and Mastercard credit cards, and by users of JCB, Visa Electron, Maestro and Solo in the UK.

The PayByCash system covers a number of different payment methods, including bank transfer, Splash Plastic and Western Union, and the cost of subscribing varies depending on the method you choose and the length of membership you require (up to 12 months membership can be added). Your membership will not be activated immediately, and the processing period will depend on the payment option you choose. If you choose to pay by Direct Debit, you can cancel your payments; otherwise the subscriptions are one-off payments. PayByCash can be used worldwide.

PayPal is a popular method of payment for those that want a secure, quick and easy way of paying for goods and services. To subscribe using this method, you will need a verified PayPal account. You are able to buy one, two, three, six or twelve months of membership with this option, and each payment is a one-off, so you will need to make a new payment to re-subscribe. PayPal is available worldwide.

You can make payments directly to Wallie instead of using PayByCash. Wallie cards can be purchased from local stores or from the Wallie website. Wallie direct payments are available in the US, Mexico and most of Europe.

You can purchase membership credit by calling the PayByPhone service and obtaining a PIN that can be redeemed on the Runescape or FunOrb website. Each PIN provides you with one month of credit and the cost of the call will be added to your telephone bill. The cost of this service will vary depending on your location and you will need to wait for 15 minutes before redeeming your PIN. This service is available in the UK, the USA, Canada, Australia, Germany, Switzerland, Austria, Ireland and New Zealand. To re-subscribe you will need to call the service to obtain a new PIN.

This system can be accessed by sending a text message to our PayBySMS service to obtain a PIN. This PIN can be redeemed on the Runescape or FunOrb website after a 15 minute delay, and each PIN provides you with one month of membership credit. If you have a contract for your mobile telephone, the cost of subscribing will be added to your bill; otherwise it will be taken from the credit on your telephone. There may also be an additional charge for the message from your network provider. The PayBySMS service is available in the UK, Austria and the USA. To re-subscribe you will need to send another message to obtain a new PIN.
