Visiting RuneScape-related sites other than www.runescape.com can potentially be dangerous to the security of both your child’s account, and their computer.

At present, the only sites which we operate are:

  • www.runescape.com
  • www.jagex.com
  • www.funorb.com

In addition to this, we have an affiliate agreements with a number of organisations. For a list of these organisations, click here

Remember that your child should never enter their RuneScape password and username on any site other than those listed above. There are websites which intentionally look very similar to our official website in order to steal passwords and account information, and this can be confusing. If there are any doubts always ensure that your child checks that the address in their browser reads www.runescape.com, or www.funorb.com before giving any information. One method of making sure of this is to add www.runescape.com or www.funorb.com to their computer’s favourites list, so they can access it directly.

Websites claiming to offer RuneScape cheats or easy ways to get ahead in the game are not being truthful. There are no cheats at all within RuneScape, and anyone who tries to convince your child otherwise is almost certainly trying to trick them.

Some sites offer computer programs which they claim can advance skills or make the RuneScape game easier. These programs are often very dangerous, and can be designed to steal personal information once they are installed on your computer. Under no circumstances should these programs be used; not only is it against the rules, but it’s also dangerous!

Occasionally, we will make content accessible to all players for a limited amount of time. This helps us to gather feedback on new content before we fully implement it. This is called a 'Beta-Test'. We never hold secret beta-tests for our players to get involved in. If there is a new game, we will always announce it our own site. Any website claiming to be RuneScape 3 – or any other Jagex game – is trying to steal your child’s password!

Jagex will never recruit Player Moderators using a website. When we send invitations to players to become Player Moderators, we only use our messaging system. There will never be any emails, or advertisements for moderator positions in game. Websites claiming to be Player Moderator recruiting centres are designed to steal your child’s account.

Visiting forums other than the official RuneScape or FunOrb forums can be a very positive experience. We are very keen that our players make friends in RuneScape, and we understand that sometimes they may wish to discuss our game in their own forums. However, it’s important to remember that forums associated with cheating or breaching the Rules of Conduct can be dangerous.

We recommend that your child reads our Scamming Prevention Guide to ensure that they know how to protect their account.
