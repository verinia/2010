Tools/Compiler Developer

Cambridge, UK

Jagex Ltd, the UK's leading independent online games studio, is looking for experienced tools programmers, who want to work with cutting-edge technology, to join our Core Technologies department. You would be working with our code libraries, game engine and bespoke tool chain.

This is an exciting opportunity for a candidate to join a fast-paced, rapidly-expanding games company based in Cambridge, and help us to achieve our ambitious goals for 2010 and beyond.

Key Duties Include:

  • Developing and maintaining our radical new web development toolset
  • Working on cutting-edge, in-house tools, languages and systems that give the studio its competitive advantage
  • Researching and developing our advanced technologies, platforms and ideas to give better gameplay to our players and performance efficiencies to our staff
  • Providing support to the users of our in-house tools and platforms
  • Supporting the production of in-house platform documentation

Essential Requirements:

  • Excellent working knowledge of Java
  • Industry experience of compiler development
  • Strong understanding/appreciation of code design philosophies and an interest in software language design
  • Good industry track record, which may include previously published products or tools development experience
  • Strong academic background at degree level or equivalent
  • The ability to think creatively to solve problems

Desirable Requirements:

  • Previous experience of working in the games industry would be a big plus

Details:

Salary
Very competitive  (depending on experience), excellent benefits package including annual bonus, private health care and gym membership.
Location
Cambridge. Candidates must be able to work in Cambridge or relocate if offered the position.
To Apply
To apply for this role . Send your CV (as a Word document), and covering letter to: careers@recruit.jagex.com.
