Parents' Guide

While our games are created with adult gamers in mind, we do appreciate that some younger players also enjoy them. We take the safety and security of these players very seriously, and we work hard to ensure that playing RuneScape is an enjoyable experience for everyone.

To find out more about what we do to ensure the online safety of our players, and for advice about account security and subscribing to our games please click here. This will re-direct you to the Jagex website, which has its own dedicated Parents’ Guide.

We have developed an additional security feature for our younger players that ensures that they cannot give out personal information. All players that admit to being under the age of 13 during account creation are restricted to using Quick Chat until their 13th birthday. Quick chat offers players a pre-defined selection of words and phrases, chosen by us, that allow them to converse with others, without being able to give out any personal details. Players that are restricted to Quick Chat are unable to type their own messages to other players.

If you would like your child to have access to our full chat features before their 13th birthday, we require you to send us a completed parental consent form. To find out more about obtaining a copy of the parental consent form, click here.
